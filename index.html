About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Michael J. Romero, a recent graduate at California State University East Bay (CSU East Bay) with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science (B.S. in CS). I am seeking challenging opportunities to further hone my problem-solving abilities, technical expertise, and leadership skills within a dynamic professional environment. I am eager to bring my enthusiasm, dedication, and strong work ethic to contribute meaningfully and achieve tangible results within the tech world.

I'm an aspiring Machine Learning (ML) enthusiast with a keen interest in Cybersecurity. I believe in the potential of ML to revolutionize our Cybersecurity space by identifying patterns of malicious threats and adapting to new attack vulnerabilities. Beyond Cybersecurity, ML is also capable of diagnosing diseases through image analysis and processing data from sensors and cameras, enabling vehicles to navigate safely in self-driving assistance.

Throughout my college career, I've engaged in a diverse range of coursework projects. These have included writing SQL scripts to design and create databases, developing Python code in Jupyter Notebook for neural network modeling that can learn XOR gates using backpropagation learning algorithms. Additionally, I have gained hands-on experience in cybersecurity and information assurance. I applied these concepts in real-world scenarios, such as capturing and analyzing encrypted and unencrypted network traffic using Wireshark, performing brute-force password attacks on Secure Shell (SSH) using Hydra, and illustrating network mapping of devices, services, and connections on a network through tools like Nmap, Zenmap, and Wireshark.

Feel free to explore my growing portfolio, and don't hesitate to reach out if you'd like to collaborate or connect about other aspirations in the ever-evolving field of technology!
